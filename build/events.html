Cooper River Distillers Petty's Island Rum Tasting

July 30, 2014: 5pm - 6:30pm

The FIRST legal distillery in Camden, NJ since before Prohibition, Cooper River Distillers JUST introduced their first product in April of 2014. Brand spanking new, their Petty's Island Rum is out and we cannot wait to share it with you!

Join us on Wednesday, July 30th from 5 to 6:30 pm for their INAUGURAL TASTING!

This will be the very first time James Yoakum, Chief Distiller, will be allowed out in fresh air to debut his new rum!

Please stop by, try the Petty's Island Rum & meet James. Bottles of rum will be available for sale and we're SURE James will be happy to officially sign any & all bottles.

Red, White and Brew presents Schnizzie

May 17, 2014

Red White and Brew proudly hosts a tasting for Schnizzie, a New Jersey made Sparkling Strawberry Vodka Cocktail. Stacy, the owner of Red White and Brew, talks about hosting tastings of small batch, unique wines, beers, and liquors. Stop by her shop in Mt. Holly, NJ to get a first hand experience!

Red, White & Brew presents Neshaminy Creek Brewing

March 26, 2014

Red, White and Brew hosted a beer tasting for one of our favorite breweries, Neshaminy Creek Brewing. Come grab a bottle of their Leons Russian Imperial Stout, infused with marshmallow fluff, bakers chocolate and crushed graham crackers. This beer is out of this world and only being sold here, at Red, White & Brew. Stop by and support your local stores and get great tasting beer at the same time.
