Featured Beer: Neshaminy Creek - Leon Russian Imperial Stout

This beer comes from right across the river in Croydon, PA, making it one of the closest production breweries to Red, White & Brew. So you KNOW the beer is fresh!

We can't keep Leon on the shelf - it's a limited production beer, that's available for only about 3 months a year. So if you can find it - stock up!

Leon Russian is a big beer. How big? They use twice as much malt than in any other beer they make. Just check out the variety of malts - Munich, Victory, Chocolate, Black Patent, and Caramel 80 along with two-row barley. And just to make it interesting, they add 45 pounds of freshly handmade marshmallow fluff made by our in house chef and Production Manager, Steve Capelli, as well as 16 and half pounds of bittersweet bakers chocolate, and 30 pounds of crumbled graham crackers to each batch. So its like a smores in a bottle! But it's not sweet - the balance comes from the 80 IBU's and the 11.6% ABV.

We really think this will be one of the best stouts you'll ever try.
