Port & Cigar Sampling

Thursday, November 13th - 5 to 7 pm

A lesson in all the different kinds of Port. Ruby, Tawny, White, LBV (Late Bottle Vintage) - which one do you prefer? This will most certainly be a great addition to your Thanksgiving table so make sure you stop by. AND, meet Jonette Kraft - owner of The Smoking Dog Cigar Shop in Maple Shade. Jonette will actually pair her cigars to go with the different ports! This is a NOT-TO-BE-MISSED event!

Chaddsford Wine Tasting & Cheese Pairing

Cordial, Aperitif & Digestif

Wednesday, November 19th - 5 to 6:30 pm

This could be one of THE most interesting tastings we've ever done.
