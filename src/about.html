About Us

Open Tuesday through Saturday from 11 am to 7 pm. Closed Sunday & Monday

"A charming and creative boutique shop specializing in interesting and eclectic wine, beer and spirits."

That's what we always envisioned and what we always wanted - a small yet full shop where special attention to selection and our customers came first. Our mission was simple: Introduce fun to those who don't know it and emphasize it to those who do!

At Red, White & Brew we weed out the mediocre stuff and search out wines, beers & liquors that are truly special and different. We want our store to be a haven to the wine lover; a destination spot for the beer maven and an oasis to those who enjoy fine spirits. We welcome special requests and we get to know our clientele - so we know what works well in our store.
